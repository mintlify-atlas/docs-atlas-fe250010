Kinoko implements all playable job branches present in MapleStory v95. Each character you create belongs to one of four branches — Explorer, Cygnus Knights, Legend, or Resistance — and advances through successive job tiers, unlocking new skills at each transition. Level caps, advancement requirements, and branch-specific mechanics (Evan's dragon, Aran's combo system, Wild Hunter's jaguar) are all handled server-side.

<Note>
  Cygnus Knights have a level cap of **120**, enforced via `ServerConfig.CYGNUS_LEVEL_MAX`. All other branches cap at level **200**.
</Note>

## Job progression overview

Job IDs follow a numeric scheme where the thousands digit encodes the branch (0 = Explorer, 1 = Cygnus Knights, 2 = Legend, 3 = Resistance) and the remaining digits encode the class category and advancement tier.

  <Tab title="Legend">

## Standard advancement levels

For non-Evan, non-Resistance jobs the advancement level thresholds are:

| Tier | Level |
|---|---|
| 1st job | 10 (Magicians: 8) |
| 2nd job | 30 (Dual Blade: 20) |
| 3rd job | 70 (Dual Blade: 55) |
| 4th job | 120 |

Resistance and Evan jobs use extended SP and their job-change level always resolves to **200** (the character's level cap) in `JobConstants.getJobChangeLevel`.

## GM and special jobs

Three internal job IDs exist for server administration and are not available to regular players:

| Job | ID |
|---|---|
| Manager | 800 |
| GM | 900 |
| Super GM | 910 |

An additional pseudo-job `ADDITIONAL_SKILLS` (ID 9000) is used internally to attach extra skill roots.
