Kinoko implements the full suite of social systems present in MapleStory v95. You can form parties to hunt together, join or found a guild to build a persistent community, link guilds into an alliance, chat in a messenger room, and maintain a friends list. All capacity limits, costs, and grade structures are defined in `GameConstants` and enforced server-side.

## Friends

Your character can maintain a friends list of up to 100 players across all channels and worlds.

| Constant | Value | Description |
|---|---|---|
| `FRIEND_MAX_BASE` | 20 | Starting friends list capacity |
| `FRIEND_MAX` | 100 | Maximum friends list size |
| `DEFAULT_FRIEND_GROUP` | `"Group Unknown"` | Default group name for new friend entries |

Friends are stored as `Friend` objects with a `FriendStatus` (online/offline/unknown) and a group name. Group names let you organize your friends list into custom categories.

<Note>
  Friend list capacity can be expanded via Cash Shop purchases up to `FRIEND_MAX` (100).
</Note>

## Parties

Parties let up to six characters share experience and cooperate on boss fights and party quests.

| Constant | Value | Description |
|---|---|---|
| `PARTY_MAX` | 6 | Maximum party members |

### Party experience bonus

When multiple party members are present in the same field, all members receive a bonus on top of the base kill experience. The bonus is calculated by `GameConstants.getPartyBonusExp`:

| Members in party | Bonus |
|---|---|
| 1 | 0% |
| 2 | 10% |
| 3 | 15% |
| 4 | 20% |
| 5 | 25% |
| 6 | 30% |

<Tip>
  The Holy Symbol skill's bonus is also affected by party size: solo use caps at +10%, while party use caps at +50% (via `GameConstants.getHolySymbolBonus`).
</Tip>

Party state is tracked via `PartyInfo` on the `User` object and the `Party` instance managed by the Central Server node. Party requests use `PartyRequestType` and results use `PartyResultType`.

## Guilds

Guilds are persistent player organizations managed by the Central Server node through the `Guild` class. Founding a guild costs meso and starts with a minimum capacity.

### Creation and costs

| Constant | Value | Description |
|---|---|---|
| `CREATE_GUILD_COST` | 5,000,000 | Meso cost to create a guild |
| `CREATE_EMBLEM_COST` | 500,000 | Meso cost to create a guild emblem |
| `DELETE_EMBLEM_COST` | 1,000,000 | Meso cost to remove a guild emblem |
| `GUILD_CAPACITY_MIN` | 10 | Starting member capacity |
| `GUILD_CAPACITY_MAX` | 100 | Maximum member capacity |

### Expanding guild capacity

You can pay meso to expand your guild's member cap. The cost depends on the current cap, from `GameConstants.getGuildExpandCost`:

| Current cap | Expansion cost |
|---|---|
| < 15 | 500,000 |
| 15 – 19 | 1,500,000 |
| 20 – 24 | 2,500,000 |
| 25 – 29 | 3,500,000 |
| 30 – 34 | 4,500,000 |
| ≥ 35 | 5,000,000 |

### Guild grades

Every guild has five grade slots. The default grade names (from `GameConstants.GUILD_GRADE_NAMES`) are:

| Grade index | Default name | `GuildRank` enum |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Master | `MASTER` |
| 2 | Jr.Master | `SUBMASTER` |
| 3 | Member | `MEMBER1` |
| 4 | Member | `MEMBER2` |
| 5 | Member | `MEMBER3` |

The Guild Master can rename any of the five grade slots. Members are sorted in the guild roster first by rank, then by level (descending).

### Guild features

- **Emblem** — A customizable badge composed of background shape, background color, foreground mark, and mark color stored as `markBg`, `markBgColor`, `mark`, `markColor` on the `Guild` object.
- **Notice** — A short text notice visible to all guild members.
- **Guild board** — A bulletin board (`GuildBoardEntry` list) with a pinned notice entry. Members can post and comment on entries.
- **Guild points** — Accumulated through play; tracked as `points` on the `Guild`.
- **Alliance membership** — A guild can belong to one alliance; the `allianceId` and `allianceName` are stored directly on the `Guild` object.

## Alliances

Guilds can form alliances to coordinate across multiple guilds.

| Constant | Value | Description |
|---|---|---|
| `CREATE_UNION_COST` | 5,000,000 | Meso cost to create a guild alliance |

Alliance requests and results are handled through `AllianceRequestType` and `AllianceResultType`. An alliance is referenced from each member guild's `allianceId` field.

---

## Messengers

The Messenger system provides a small private chat room for a handful of players.

| Constant | Value | Description |
|---|---|---|
| `MESSENGER_MAX` | 3 | Maximum members per messenger room |

Messenger state is managed through the `Messenger` class (on the Central Server node) and `MessengerUser` objects. Each `User` holds a `messengerId` field indicating their current room. Room creation, invitation, and leave operations are handled by `MessengerRequest` and `MessengerProtocol`.

---

## Rankings

The `RankManager` on the Central Server node periodically recomputes world rankings. Rankings are used for the leaderboard visible in the in-game ranking UI and for guild GP standings.
